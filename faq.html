Frequently Asked Questions

Salon Hours

Monday-Friday: 9:00AM-9:00PM
Saturday and Sunday: 9:00AM-7:00PM

The Arman Salon team is not available on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas, and New Year's Day.

Order Processing

Once your order is placed, it generally takes 1-2 business days to process and pick. For expedited or overnight shipping, orders placed before 12:00 PM PT, Monday through Friday, will typically ship the same day.

Shipping Notifications

We will email you a shipment notification along with a tracking number as soon as your package ships.

Contact Us

Returns Policy

U.S. returns are free within 30 days. Note you are allowed 1 exchange on your original order. International orders are final—no returns or exchanges. Shipping and duties are non-refundable.

Appointment Cancellations

We kindly ask that you give at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid a cancellation fee.

Booking Appointments

We recommend booking an appointment to ensure availability, but we also accept walk-ins based on stylist availability.

No Additional Fees

No, there will be no additional charges if you select this mode of payment or even PayPal payment option.

Payment Methods

Arman Salon accepts online payment such as PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, and Credit Card. For those who do not want to pay online, Money and Bank transfer are also available.
