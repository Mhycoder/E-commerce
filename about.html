History of Arman Salon

It was in 1978 when Arman came to the Philippines. He was the manager of Rever Salon at the Mandarin Oriental Hotel, and after becoming a partner and later on its sole owner, Arman expanded the Rever Salon to five locations. In 1989, after seeing a need for a salon that would offer high quality hairdressing at a customer-friendly price, Arman established Arman Salon Inc. With the desire to share his expertise to more Filipinos, he opened the very first Arman Salon in New Farmer's Market in Cubao, Quezon City.
